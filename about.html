About Talanta Fountain

By getting to know us, you will understand that we are dedicated to empower youth talents

Who We Are

Talanta Fountain is a talent support enterprise registered under the Companies Act 2018. This means that we are that Organization that address a basic unmet need & solves social problems through a talent-drive approach.

Our incorporation was inspired by the need to work with youth, aged 10 to 35 years, across Africa with a view to creating a productive generation for a prosperous continent. The organization majorly deals with talent mobilization, training, capacity building and Consultancy in the areas of community development with special focus to youth empowerment.

What We Stand By

Vision

To create an equitable society through youth economic empowerment.

Mission

To enable an inclusive economic growth through talent and entrepreneurial skills development, inspired by innovation and technology.

Our Position

Developing a productive generation for prosperity.
